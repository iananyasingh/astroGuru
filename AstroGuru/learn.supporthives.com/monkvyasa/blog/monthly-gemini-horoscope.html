Monthly Gemini Horoscope

13.09.2021

This is going to be a big month for all Geminis as both the new and full moons will be active in the most important areas of your charts, ruling all of the key areas of your life including home, family and career.

The new moon will see you initially divert your attention to your home and family. The positioning of both the sun and the new moon make now the perfect time for you to embark on a new creative project at home.

This could be finding a new house, buying or renting a holiday home or refreshing your current space. Any plans like this at this time will proceed very smoothly and successfully.

Your immediate family members also feature in this important part of your chart, and this will be a good time for you to discover new and improved ways of connecting with your family.

During the first half of this month you may have to travel some distance to be able to achieve some of these plans, so keep an eye out for any upcoming journeys which seem to orient around property or family.

These plans may also require you to make a big decision at some point this month, and if you do make sure you do some added reflection before committing to anything big.
